Target Audience

The target audience for this weather website is for anyone who has any need to look up the weather for any reason. Weather that reason be curiosity or to see what the weather will be like for a planned trip.

Persona One

Joey Fake

  • Retired
  • 60 year old
  • Lives together with his wife
  • Lots of grandchildren
  • Loves to take his family camping

Persona Two

Karen Faker

  • Single Mother
  • Two children
  • Works as a middle school teacher
  • Wants to learn how to do outdoor things for her kids. (Hiking, camping, fishing, etc.)

Scenario One

Can you see what the area might look like?

The weather site will provide a gallery for the areas in which the weather provides.

Scenario Two

Should we bring rain gear camping?

The weather site will help determine proper gear for outdoor activities.

Scenario Three

What is the traffic like?

The weather site will give an idea on what traffic will be like depending in the area.

Scenario Four

How will I know of potential Storms?

The weather site will provide an insight into oncoming storms or potential storms.

Scenario Five

What kind of activities are available?

Activites are dependent on the area and the current weather. Most outdoor activites will help be be determined through weather knowledge.
