Coast Tour

Mexico’s largest inhabited island, Cozumel boasts pristine Caribbean beaches and spectacular coral life. On this exclusive jungle and snorkeling tour, you’ll get a taste of the island’s ancient culture and natural beauty behind the wheel of your own private jeep—with time to savor local delicacies. Maximize your visit by covering all the island's must-see sites in one visit. Meet your guide in your hotel lobby and, at the wheel of your own jeep, follow your guide to ancient Mayan settlements and a coral-filled underwater park. Your guide will fill you in on the powerful civilization that once ruled over the area before you hit the beach. Cozumel’s colorful corals are part of the world’s second largest barrier reef. Strap on a snorkeling mask for a first-hand look at the island’s stunning marine scenery. You’ll have time to lounge on Cozumel’s eastern sand beaches, famous for their warm turquoise waters, and enjoy an authentic lunch before returning to your hotel. You can also customize your itinerary to include optional upgrades like visits to San Gervasio archaeological site and Punta Sur National Park, or activities like scuba diving, horseback riding, parasailing, or swimming with dolphins. Ruins Tour

Discover the ancient history of Mexico on a day trip from Cozumel to the archaeological site of Tulum. On a ferry to the mainland, take in views of the coastline that most visitors never get to experience. Once at Tulum, your guide gives context to unmarked points of interest that are easy to miss if visiting on your own. Lunch, transportation, and admission tickets are included.Meet your tour operator at a pier in downtown Cozumel, where you board a ferry bound for mainland Mexico. After a pleasant ride with coastal views rarely seen by visitors, arrive in Playa del Carmen. An air-conditioned minivan brings you directly to the gates of Tulum. Follow your guide on a walking tour of the ancient city perched on an oceanside cliff. Going with a guide ensures you understand the layout of the archaeological site and don’t miss any unmarked points of interest. Pause for photos of the temples and castles with a backdrop of the Caribbean Sea. After your tour, enjoy lunch with the group and then head back to Playa del Carmen to catch the ferry. Private Tour

Plan your own sightseeing itinerary while on vacation on beautiful Cozumel. Travel in the comfort of a private vehicle with your own personal guide. This private customizable tour is designed for 4 hours of sightseeing at island attractions such as the Municipal Market, San Gervasio archaeological ruins and Chankanaab Beach Adventure Park. Pricing is based on four passengers per vehicle, including round-trip transport. Your experienced guide will get to know you and make suggestions to build your perfect tour. All our vehicles are equipped with A/C for your comfort. Places you may visit: -City tour of Historical Monuments. -Shopping time in town. -Beach time in one of our private beach clubs -Go to Snorkeling to Sky Reef or Tortugas Snorkel Center -Tour the east coast of the island with stops at different beautiful spots for marvelous photo opportunities and/or lunch as; -Sr Iguanas -Mezcalitos -Punta Morena -Chen Rio -Coconuts -Playa San Martin -El Mirador -Mojito Bar -Rastas Reggae Bar. Additional destinations you can visit for a nominal entrance fee: -The Chocolate Factory -Sabores De Cozumel park -Mayan Village theme park -The Mayan Kao Kao Factory -Discovery Mexico park -Chankanaab -San Gervasio Ruins -Punta Sur Park -El Cedral Town This is your opportunity to design a tour to do what you want, when you want and stay as long as you want!
